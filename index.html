ABOUT US

Delivering Exceptional Medical Solutions

Founded in 2024, Relacom Healthcare is dedicated to delivering exceptional medical solutions, including medicines, surgical disposables, laboratory equipment, x-ray equipment, and electromedical devices. With extensive experience in the healthcare industry, we pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence and continuous improvement. Our mission is to enhance the quality of healthcare in the country by expanding our business and services, ultimately ensuring the best possible care for our clients.

Our Services

Relacom Healthcare provides the highest quality medical supplies, devices, and equipment for both private and public sectors. Our extensive range of products and services includes:

Medical Equipment

We offer a wide variety of medical equipment such as radiology machines, ICU equipment, operation room assemblies, and nursery equipment.

Healthcare Items and Devices

Our healthcare product line includes various medical items and devices essential for patient care and treatment.

Medical Consumables

We supply medical consumables like syringes, cannulas, needles, and more to ensure that healthcare providers have the necessary tools for their daily operations.

Laboratories and Blood Banking:

We provide laboratory and blood banking devices, reagents, and consumables to support accurate diagnostics and safe blood storage.

Pharmaceuticals

We distribute and promote pharmaceutical products, cosmetic products, and health-consuming products to meet the diverse needs of our clients.

Custom Solutions

We work closely with our clients to develop custom solutions tailored to their specific needs, ensuring efficient and effective healthcare operations.
